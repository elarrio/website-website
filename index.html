About Emilio

Emilioats, or better known as Emilio is a Japanese/Dominican musician, composer, beatmaker. Based in Georgia, he is known for his lush sampled beats across many mediums. Paying close attention to the groove and melodies to ensure replay ability amongst all levels. He first started from SoundCloud, posting many anime/jazz inspired beats. Eventually moving on towards composing piano and guitar centric music. His takeoff arised from his debut album “Staying Indoors”. Amassing over eight hundred thousand streams, Emilio continued to enhance this sound into later tracks gaining a small rise in popularity towards the Lofi-Hiphop community. Taking inspiration from many genres, including folk music, jazz and early hip-hop, his emergence in beats suddenly gained attention from many popular music labels. Fans were pleased to listen, often noting, “it's the sounds you hear when you just need to relax and unwind”.
